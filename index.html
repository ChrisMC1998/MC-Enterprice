Tips & Notes
🔑 Key Conflicts
Custom shortcuts can't use N or / — those are reserved. Keys are case-insensitive.
✅ When Active
Shortcuts only fire when you're not typing in an input, select, or textarea field — so they won't interfere with data entry.
💾 Saved Locally
Custom shortcuts are saved in your browser's local storage and persist between sessions on this device.
PIN Lock
Adds a lock screen to this app on this device/browser. This is a local convenience lock, not real account security — anyone with direct access to the browser's storage (e.g. via DevTools) can still bypass it. Use it to stop casual access, not to protect against a determined attacker with device access.

Data Storage & Local-First Architecture
This ERP system operates entirely on a local-first principle. All business data — including transactions, customers, inventory, invoices, and settings — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your device.
✅ What We DO
  • Store all data locally on your device only
  • Allow you to export/backup your own data
  • Give you full control to delete all data
  • Operate fully offline after first load
❌ What We DON'T DO
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use cookies for tracking or analytics
  • Share data with third parties
  • Collect telemetry or usage statistics
